This is it! [[The Last Battle]]()[^narnia]. Kronos and his army of titans, monsters, gods, and demigods is about to reach New York City and Mount Olympus. And, because of a rampaging Titan coming across the country, the city is almost entirely undefended. 

[^narnia]: And I enjoyed the Last Olympian far more than the final Narnia book personally.

So it's up to Percy and the demigods of Camp Half-Blood to save the day!

Oh, did I mention that Percy is about ot turn 16 in this book? And that there's a certain prophecy all about that? 

It's quite a book. It ends as you'd expect, with the good guys saving the day, but there are quite a few crazy battles, big sacrifices, and truly dangerous moments along the way.

There is so much action in this book, it's hard to put it down. 

I think my favorite moment though, comes in the ending. In saving the day, Percy is, as it goes in these stories, owed a boon by the gods. He could choose anything. Even to ascend to minor godhood himself. So of course...

Well, you'll just have to read the story and find out!

It's a great end to the first series. I'm so glad that there are more in this same universe, both continuing in [[The Heroes of Olympus]](), but also branching out to Egyptian mythology in [[todo:The Kane Chronicles]]() (which I'll reread) and Norse mythology in [[wiki:Magnus Chase and the Gods of Asgard]]() (new to me!).

So much to look forward to!

Onward!
