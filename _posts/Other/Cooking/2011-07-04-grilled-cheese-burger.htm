categories: - Other/Cooking comments: true date: 2011-07-04 04:55:55 layout: post title: Grilled-cheese-burger --- Grilled cheese? Cheeseburger? You decide! @embed{2011.07.03-grilled-cheese-burger.jpg} If you have a Foreman Grill, you owe it to yourself to try this out:
  • grill the burger in the Foreman Grill or in a skillet
  • make a grilled cheese with at least two slices of American cheese
  • peel apart the grilled cheese and insert the burger (and any condiments--I'm partial to spicy brown mustard)
Enjoy!
