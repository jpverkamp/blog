categories: - Other/Cooking comments: true date: 2011-01-17 04:55:17 layout: post title: Homemade tortilla chips --- We were in a frying sort of mood after @crosslink["corn-breaded-pickle-chips"]{yesterday's pickle chips}, so today we decided to make some homemade tortilla chips. It's actually a really simple process:
  • cut up a tortilla
  • fry the pieces
Who'da thunk? @embed{2011.01.16-homemade-tortilla-chips.jpg} I wish I'd gotten a few more pictures while we were still making them, but it turns out that they're too tasty. We were eating them faster than I could fry more. Really, we're lucky that we got even this shot. If you ever have the time (and you don't need much) and spare tortillas, I really would suggest you try it. It's amazing to have chips and salsa, all homemade.
