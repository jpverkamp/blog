categories: - Other/Cooking comments: true date: 2012-07-30 04:55:33 layout: post title: Chicken and tomatoes --- Today we have one of those simple recipes when you're short on prep time but still want something delicious. You'll need:
  • chicken
  • rice
  • tomatoes
  • herbs and spices (we used basil and oregano)
  • olive oil
Basically, go ahead and start the rice. While that's cooking, just put everything down in layers in something bake-with-able. @embed{2012.07.29-chicken-and-tomatoes-1.jpg} Stick it in the oven and about twenty minutes later... bam.
