Today I wanted to take a chance to give everyone a chance to actually play the game. The rules are simple. There are three six-sided dice available: Red: 2, 2, 4, 4, 9, 9 Green: 1, 1, 6, 6, 8, 8 Blue: 3, 3, 5, 5, 7, 7 You choose one die and then I'll choose one. We'll each roll our die twenty times; for each roll, whoever comes out ahead scores a point. If you get ten or more points, you win (so you win ties). If you don't make ten, I win.
