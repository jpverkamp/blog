Title: The Last John Smith

Premise: Among a thousand parallel realities, everyone has a thousand twins. Except for John Smith. Someone has been systematically killing all of the alternate John Smiths on all of the worlds.@footnote{And since I write by the seat of my pants... That's all even I know thus far.}

Eventually, I'll probably publish the first chapter online. If anyone wants to read the story as I go or after I'm done though, just email me. I'd be happy to have more beta readers.
